Privacy Policy — AI Usage Meter
Last updated: March 2026
What we collect
AI Usage Meter collects anonymous usage data when you interact with supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity). This includes:

A randomly generated user ID (no name, email, or account info)
Prompt metadata: length, token estimate, language, domain classification, prompt type
Response metadata: token estimate, latency, streaming duration
Session data: session ID, prompt count, time between prompts
UI signals: whether regenerate or suggested prompts were used, file/image attachments detected
Environmental estimates: estimated energy, CO₂, and water usage per prompt
Browser environment: browser type/version, OS, viewport size, timezone offset
AI model used

What we do NOT collect

The text of your prompts or responses
Any personally identifiable information
Account information from any AI platform

How data is used
Collected data is sent to our server (dev.madebyshu.net) and stored in a database. It is used solely to provide usage statistics and environmental impact estimates back to you via the extension.
Data sharing
We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
Data retention
Data is associated with your anonymous user ID. You can download your dataset at any time via the extension.
Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance.
